About

Hi, I'm Shekeyla.

Longmeadow Pet Care Provider  · 

Pets have always been a big part of my life. Dogs are deeply important to me — I see them for the intelligent, emotional beings they are. They deserve all the love, attention, and nourishment we can give, and that is what I strive to provide in every visit and every stay.

Every pet in my care is treated with love like my very own fur baby. I enjoy learning about every animal's personality and quirks. I have house-trained multiple puppies, and cared for PearlJam, my senior rescue with complex medical needs — deaf, vision loss, and lung disease — for over four years. I currently have a 5-year-old Goldendoodle, Pretzel, who loves making new friends.

You can be assured that your pet will be treated positively and cared for preciously.

What to Expect

A Typical Day

Active & Energetic Dogs

Energetic dogs will get the physical activity they need with outside walks and jogs and plenty of play. I enjoy daily walks and jogs with pets that benefit from the activity — and I genuinely enjoy those walks too.

Calm & Cuddly Dogs

For the lovey-dovey pets, they are most welcome to accompany me right on my lap or in a cozy bed next to my desk as I work. Pups with calmer spirits will get all the cuddle time they can manage.

What I Need to Know

If your dog has any special needs or instructions, please feel comfortable sharing that with me in great detail. I want your pet to feel comfortable — and that starts with knowing what their experience is like at home.

Updates & Communication

Every booking includes photo updates so you always know your dog is happy and well. I aim to respond quickly and keep you in the loop throughout every visit or stay — no radio silence, ever.

House Rules

Policies

Vaccinations

All pets must be current on core vaccinations. Proof may be requested before the first visit.

Cancellations

Boarding cancellations follow a three-day policy. Other services: full refund with 48+ hours notice; non-refundable within 48 hours.

Dog Size & Capacity

I can host dogs 0–40 lbs. Up to 2 pets per stay. Larger dogs welcome for walking and drop-in visits.

Potty Breaks

Regular outdoor breaks throughout the day. I work from home and am generally available all day to attend to your pet's needs.

Special Needs

Medication administration (oral and injectable), senior dog care, and special needs experience are all within my capabilities.

Emergencies

In the event of a veterinary emergency, I will contact you immediately and seek care on your pet's behalf if you are unreachable.
