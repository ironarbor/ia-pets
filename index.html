About

Hi, I'm Shekeyla.

Pet Care Provider  · 
Based in Longmeadow, MA

Pets have always been a big part of my life. Dogs are deeply important to me — I see them for the intelligent, emotional beings they are. They deserve all the love, attention, and nourishment we can give, and that is what I strive to provide in every visit and every stay.

Every pet in my care is treated with love like my very own fur baby. I enjoy learning about every animal's personality and quirks. I have house-trained multiple puppies, and cared for PearlJam, my senior rescue with complex medical needs — deaf, vision loss, and lung disease — for over four years. I currently have a 5-year-old Goldendoodle, Pretzel, who loves making new friends.

You can be assured that your pet will be treated positively and cared for preciously.
